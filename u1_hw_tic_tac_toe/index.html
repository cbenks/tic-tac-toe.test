Tic-Tac-Toe

Instructions

  • You will have either X's or O's depending on your turn.
  • Turns alternate.
  • Select one of the 9 areas in the grid and click it to place your X or O.
  • Your goal is to connect 3 of your X's or O's in a line horizontally, vertically or diagonally while simultaneously preventing your opponent from doing so. Good luck!
